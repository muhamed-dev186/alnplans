Professional & Modern Builders in Kampala

National Estate's and Construction Company (ALN) is a leading construction firm based in Ntinda Kigowa Rd, Kampala. We specialize in transforming architectural dreams into tangible realities.

Our core philosophy is that modern buildings should be "thought through." This means we don't just stack bricks; we analyze the environment, the usage, and the budget to create structures that stand the test of time and look beautiful.

What We Do

Comprehensive Services

We offer end-to-end construction solutions tailored to your specific needs and budget.

Architectural Design

Renovations
